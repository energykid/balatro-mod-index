SuperFine!! is a 'Vanilla++' mod - aiming to add content that fits in with vanilla's balance, but stands out on its own.

Currently, it adds:
22 Jokers,
5 Boss Blinds,
two Enhancements,
one Tarot card,
2 Spectral cards,
one deck,
and an Attunement system - letting you upgrade your Jokers!
